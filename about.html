Education

Knowledge I've Acquired

My educational journey has been instrumental in shaping my career success. I'm proud to have earned a Master of Business Administration in HR Strategic Management from Brigham Young University. As a recipient of the Ivan C. Smith scholarship worth $10,000, I was honored to be recognized for my academic achievements and dedication to excellence.

Prior to that, I completed my undergraduate studies at Brigham Young University-Hawaii in Laie, HI, where I earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Management with a focus on HR and Organizational Behavior, along with a minor in Economics. With a commendable GPA of 3.62 and being awarded three scholarships, including the highest full-tuition scholarship, I was privileged to be recognized for my academic excellence and commitment to learning.

Overall, my education has provided me with a strong foundation of knowledge and skills that have been instrumental in my professional achievements and contributions in the field of HR. I'm grateful for the opportunities I've had to learn, grow, and make a meaningful impact, and I'm excited to continue my journey of lifelong learning in the HR field.
